Our Story

The story of The Craftsman Legacy begins in Moradabad — a city renowned worldwide for its centuries-old metal craftsmanship. Surrounded by skilled artisans and generations of expertise, we were inspired to create a brand that honors traditional craftsmanship while bringing it into modern interiors.

What began as admiration for the artistry of handcrafted metalwork gradually evolved into a commitment to preserve these skills. Every piece we create reflects the dedication of artisans who shape, polish, and finish brass by hand.

Craftsmanship

At The Craftsman Legacy, craftsmanship is more than a process — it is a story of patience, precision, and pride. Our artisans combine traditional hand techniques with modern finishing methods to create décor pieces that are elegant and timeless.

Every product is carefully inspected to ensure it meets our standards of quality, durability, and design excellence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a globally respected handcrafted brand that represents the true essence of Indian metal artistry.

  • Preserve and promote traditional craftsmanship
  • Empower artisan communities through sustainable work
  • Create designs blending heritage with modern aesthetics
  • Deliver products that represent authenticity and longevity
